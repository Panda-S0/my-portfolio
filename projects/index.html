Chatroom

a social messaging app developed with React Native, enabling users to create accounts and connect with others through seamless real-time chat. The app features secure sign-up and login functionalities, ensuring a safe and private experience. Users can engage in one-on-one conversations, join group chats, and customize their profiles. With a clean, intuitive interface and instant message delivery, Chatroom provides a fast and reliable way for people to communicate and stay connected.

Food To Home

a feature-rich food delivery app built with React Native, allowing users to browse a variety of restaurants and select from diverse menus. Customers can customize their orders, track real-time delivery status, and view the driver's live location on an interactive map. With a seamless user experience, secure cash-on-delivery payment, and an integrated chat feature for direct communication between buyers and sellers, Food to Home ensures a convenient and reliable way to enjoy restaurant-quality meals from the comfort of home.

Poster

a social networking app developed with React Native and JavaScript, designed for users to review and rate products. The app includes comprehensive sign-up and login functionalities, enabling users to create accounts, rate products, and write detailed reviews. "Poster" fosters a community of informed consumers sharing their opinions and experiences.
