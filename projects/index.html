Food To Home

a feature-rich food delivery app built with React Native, allowing users to browse a variety of restaurants and select from diverse menus. Customers can customize their orders, track real-time delivery status, and view the driver's live location on an interactive map. With a seamless user experience, secure cash-on-delivery payment, and an integrated chat feature for direct communication between buyers and sellers, Food to Home ensures a convenient and reliable way to enjoy restaurant-quality meals from the comfort of home.

Falling Sand

an innovative application written in Python that simulates the mesmerizing effect of sand falling. Users can pause the simulation, draw various shapes, and watch as the sand interacts with their drawings, creating intricate and beautiful patterns. This app provides a relaxing and visually captivating experience.

Blind Cat

a stealth and precision-based game developed in the Godot game engine. Players take on the role of a blind cat navigating a dark environment, relying on sound to locate and catch elusive chickens. Each level introduces new challenges, from trickier terrains to faster chickens, making strategy and timing key to success. The game's atmospheric design and unique mechanics create an immersive experience that tests players' skills and instincts.
